About Us

Everybody faces fascinating work experiences everyday, but these golden stories are locked away due to corporate confidentiality. By removing the identity and context from these stories, we can share all our experiences and learn from one another how we improvised or dealt with different situations! Let's have a good laugh, and improve ourselves at the same time!

The more stories we have the further we go, tell us your experience today!
